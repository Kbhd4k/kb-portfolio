NEEDLE.

The system behind the portfolio — an editorial, high-contrast language built on Bebas Neue, Epilogue, and DM Mono, anchored by a single decisive red. Documented here so every page stays consistent. Flip the toggle to see both themes.

01

PRINCIPLES

BIG TYPE, FEW WORDS

Oversized display headlines carry the page. Copy stays lean — every word earns its place.

Voice
ONE LOUD COLOR

Neutral greys do the work; a single red marks what matters. Accent is a scalpel, not a coat of paint.

Color
STRUCTURE SHOWS

Hairline rules, mono labels, and split layouts make the grid legible. Order is part of the aesthetic.
